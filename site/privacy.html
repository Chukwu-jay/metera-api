Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Overview

Metera helps users save and reuse AI workflow context. During beta, Metera is designed around explicit user action: the browser extension composes, inserts, captures, and saves only when the user clicks the relevant control.

Information We Process

Depending on how you use Metera, we may process:

  • account email and workspace membership information;
  • API requests routed through the Metera control plane;
  • workflow names, captures, summaries, context packs, and related metadata you choose to save;
  • cache outcome, policy, usage, cost, and diagnostic metadata;
  • browser extension settings, local permissions, and local capture previews stored in your browser.

Browser Extension Data

The Metera Browser Bridge does not silently scrape provider pages and does not auto-submit prompts. Captured text is staged locally for review and is sent to Metera only after an explicit save action.

How We Use Information

  • to provide workflow memory and prompt composition;
  • to operate exact, semantic, and shadow cache features;
  • to enforce policy, namespace, tenant, and workspace boundaries;
  • to provide savings, usage, and operational visibility;
  • to debug, secure, and improve the beta product.

Retention And Deletion

Browser-local capture previews can be cleared by the user. Saved workflow content is retained in Metera until deleted or until a beta retention process removes it. During beta, contact support for deletion requests.
